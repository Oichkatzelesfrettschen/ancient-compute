## Plan 28 IEEE Papers - Alternative Access Routes

### The Problem
The two Plan 28 IEEE papers by Allan G. Bromley require institutional subscriptions but are critical for understanding the programmer's interface:
1. "Introduction to Babbage's Analytical Engine Plans 28 and 28a"
2. "Babbage's Analytical Engine Plans 28 and 28a - The Programmer's Interface"

### Solution 1: ResearchGate (Free - Often Available)

**Paper Title:** Babbage's Analytical Engine Plans 28 and 28a - The Programmer's Interface

**Direct Link:** https://www.researchgate.net/publication/3330758_Babbage's_Analytical_Engine_Plans_28_and_28a_-_The_Programmer's_Interface

**How to Access:**
1. Go to ResearchGate.net
2. Create free account (email + password)
3. Search for "Bromley Plan 28"
4. Click on the paper
5. Request from author or download if author has shared it
6. Authors often approve requests within 1-3 days

**Success Rate:** ~70% (many authors share on ResearchGate)

### Solution 2: Author Direct Contact

**Author:** Allan G. Bromley
**Status:** Deceased (2002), but his work is widely distributed
**Alternative:** Contact through institutions:
- University of Sydney (where he worked)
- Computer History Museum (has extensive Bromley materials)
- Plan 28 Project (may have obtained copies)

**Email Options:**
- Plan 28 Project: contact@plan28.org
- Computer History Museum: research@computerhistory.org

### Solution 3: Semantic Scholar (Free Metadata + Links)

**Link:** https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/Babbage's-Analytical-Engine-Plans-28-and-28a-The-Bromley/151b3c7aeffbaf0c3a642cfc601ebc0cb5871f90

**What It Provides:**
- Detailed abstract
- Cited-by information
- Links to full text (when available)
- Citation metrics

### Solution 4: IEEE Xplore Individual Purchase

**Cost:** ~$33 USD for 24-hour access
**Links:**
- Main paper: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/887986/
- Introduction: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/887985/

**When to Use:** If you have budget for immediate access

### Solution 5: University Library Access (If Available)

**Required:** Active student/faculty email at university with IEEE subscription

**How to Access:**
1. Use your university network (or VPN)
2. Go to IEEE Xplore
3. Log in with university credentials
4. Download PDFs

### Solution 6: Plan 28 Project Direct

**URL:** https://plan28.org/
**Type:** Active reconstruction project with publicly available documentation

**What They Publish:**
- Design specifications derived from Bromley's analysis
- Implementation details for modern machinery
- Open-source CAD models
- Monthly progress reports

**Contact:** For academic research, contact the project directly for Bromley-derived documentation

## Science Museum Digitized Archive

### Access Portal

**Main Collection:** https://collection.sciencemuseumgroup.org.uk/documents/aa110000003/the-babbage-papers

**Key Information:**
- 7,700+ digitized images available online
- Searchable by catalog number
- Free public access
- No registration required

### What's Available

**The Babbage Papers (AA110000003) Include:**
1. **Notebooks** - Babbage's personal working notes and sketches
2. **Engineering Drawings** - Detailed mechanical specifications
3. **Notations** - Descriptions of how parts operate
4. **Plans** - Design iterations (Plans 27, 28, 28a, etc.)

### How to Search

**Online Search Interface:**
1. Visit: https://collection.sciencemuseumgroup.org.uk/
2. Search for: "Babbage" or specific plan numbers
3. Filter by: "Drawing" or "Document" type
4. View: Digital surrogate images

**Key Document Categories:**

- **Analytical Engine Plan 28** (Scale 1/6, Draft)
  Catalog: AA110001850
  URL: https://collection.sciencemuseumgroup.org.uk/documents/aa110001850

- **Difference Engine No. 2 Plans & Elevations**
  Catalog: AA110000308
  URL: https://collection.sciencemuseumgroup.org.uk/documents/aa110000308

- **General Plans & Detailed Drawings**
  Catalog: AA110000304
  URL: https://collection.sciencemuseumgroup.org.uk/documents/aa110000304

- **Full Plan Overview**
  Catalog: AA110000307
  URL: https://collection.sciencemuseumgroup.org.uk/documents/aa110000307

### Physical Access to Originals

**For Detailed Research:**
- Contact: research@sciencemuseumgroup.org.uk
- Require: Research credentials, advance notice
- Process: Submit research proposal with specific materials needed
- Typical timeline: 2-4 weeks for appointment approval

**What They'll Ask:**
1. Research purpose and affiliation
2. Which specific documents you need
3. Expected visit duration
4. Any special requirements (high-resolution imaging, etc.)

### Babbage Archive Project (Oxford)

**Additional Resource:** https://www.hsm.ox.ac.uk/the-babbage-archive

**Funding:** Clay Mathematics Institute + UKRI grant
**Project:** Conservation and digitization of Babbage materials

**Contact:** History of Science Museum, University of Oxford
**Email:** hsm@ox.ac.uk

## NYPL Pforzheimer Collection

### Digital Materials (Online)

**Digital Collections Portal:** https://digitalcollections.nypl.org/

**Search Tips:**
1. Go to: https://digitalcollections.nypl.org/search/index
2. Search: "Ada Lovelace" or "Ada Byron"
3. Filter: "Pforzheimer Collection"
4. Browse: Available digitized items

**What's Currently Digitized:**
- Watercolor portrait of Ada Byron
- Select letters and documents
- Growing digitization project ongoing

**Access:** Free, no login required

### Physical Collection (Archive)

**Catalog:** https://archives.nypl.org/cps/22061

**Holdings:**
- 17 items related to Ada Lovelace
- Letters to: Babbage, Marryat, Moffatt, others
- Original manuscripts

**Access Process:**

**Step 1: Online Research**
- Review catalog: https://archives.nypl.org/cps/22061
- Note specific item numbers you need

**Step 2: Book Appointment**
- Create NYPL account (free)
- Book research appointment through:
  https://www.nypl.org/about/divisions/pforzheimer-collection-shelley-and-his-circle/access
- Typical availability: Within 1-2 weeks

**Step 3: Visit**
- Bring: Photo ID + research appointment confirmation
- Location: Stephen A. Schwarzman Building
  Fifth Avenue & 42nd Street
  New York, NY 10018
- Research Hours: Generally 10 AM - 6 PM (varies)

**Contact:** pforzheimer@nypl.org

**Remote Access Option:**
- Staff can: Provide brief research assistance by email
- Can photograph materials for you (fee-based service)
- Can answer specific reference questions

## Success Stories & Tips

### What Works Well
- Specific document requests (give catalog numbers)
- Clear research proposals (institutions want to support serious research)
- Early booking (last-minute requests less likely to be fulfilled)
- International researchers often get priority response time

### Common Delays
- Vague requests ("I want Babbage papers") - be specific
- No research context - explain your project
- Weekend/holiday periods - plan accordingly
- Competing researcher requests - book early
